Sharks: The big fish of the sea. However, not all sharks are the same. There's a lot of sharks out there, but lets get the simple facts straight. Sharks can be identified by their gill count of 5-7 slits and their skeleton which is made of cartilage. With more than 500 known shark species, there are many unique (shark) fish in the sea. However, when you think shark, you think of the shark attacks on humans. This has caused an irrational fear of sharks and have been conceived as monsters of the ocean but they aren't nearly as dangerous as they are portayed in movies and media.
